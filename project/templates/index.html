Advance in class
communication

Simpleclass brings about a learning
management system to improve the
sharing of information between lecturers and their students.

What is this website?

This website is a platform that allows lecturers to upload
course materials for students, manage assignments and
proposals submitted by his or her students and to provide
feedback all in one place.
